UniSearch Information

About

UniSearch is all about helping teens choose the right college that they want to go to. This website mainly targets teens that are about to enter the phase where they have to choose a college if they decide to go.

Plans

In the future we plan on updating the site so that it has a map to show the colleges that are in your area, and we plan on showing some of the scholarships, majors, and tuition of the schools on the site.

Who We Are

The creators of this site are just some teens that are in high school that both struggle with the colleges that we want to go to.

How We Plan To Make Profit

To make profit, we plan to put advertisements on websites like Instagram, FaceBook, and Twitter to get teens interested in our website do they can browse our site and look into some of the colleges that that this site shows them.

Inspiration

The inspiration for this website came from one of the creators that is a junior and struggles with picking a college because he is indecisive and can't choose a college.
